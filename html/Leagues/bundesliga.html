The German Bundesliga
stands 4th in the top leagues table.
It stands as the top tier league
in Germany and is the
primary football competition there.
The Bundesliga comprises 18 teams and
operates on a system of promotion and relegation,
seasons run from August to May,
most games are played on Saturdays and Sundays,
with a few games played on weekdays.

Bundesliga RECORDS

Bayern Munich has always been dominating Bundesliga, they have been crowned Bundesliga Champions 31 times. Once, they won the League Title 9 times, straight in a row, they were literally on a winning spree.

Bayern Munich's Legend, Gerd Muller (German), has scored 365 goals in 427 Bundesliga Matches, earning the all time top goal scorer title in Bundesliga's History.

Bayern Munich's Goalkeeper, Manuel Neuer has 210 cleansheets in 455 bundesliga matches, which makes him the Bundesliga Cleansheet leader of all time, he is considered to be the best goalkeeper playing right now.

Lucas Hernandez (French) was signed by Bayern Munich for 80 Million Euros, he is the most expensive signing in the Bundesliga ever and the 3rd most expensive defender in the world.
