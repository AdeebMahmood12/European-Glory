La Liga RECORDS

Real Madrid have won the LaLiga for an outrageous 35 times. Due to the numerous League Titles, and Continental Trophies, Real Madrid CF is considered to be the Best Club in the World.

FC Barcelona's gem Lionel Messi (Argentine) has scored 474 goals in 520 LaLiga matches, creating him the all time topscorer in LaLiga's history.

Valencia CF's Andoni Zubizarreta (Spanish) has 235 cleansheets in 620 matches, this achievement makes him a place at the top of the LaLiga Cleansheets table.

Philippe Coutinho (Brazillian) was signed by FC Barcelona to fill up the gap Neymar Jr's departure caused. They signed him for a whopping 160 Million Euros, causing him to become the most expensive player ever bought into LaLiga.
