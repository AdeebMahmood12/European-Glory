PREMIER LEAGUE RECORDS

Manchester United have won this prestigious title, 20 times since 1889, the Red Devils's success was highly contributed by the undoubtedly best coach of all time, Sir Alex Ferguson (Scottish).

Newcastle United's Legend, Alan Shearer (English) has 260 goals in 441 Premier League matches, he has scored the most goals in the history of Premier League.

Peter Cech (Czech) has 207 cleansheets in 443 Premier League Matches, which puts him at the top of the Premier League cleansheet table.

Jack Grealish (English) was signed by Manchester City from a fellow Premier League Club, Aston Villa for an outrageous 100 Million Pound transfer fee, making him the most expensive transfer in the Premier League.
