Serie a RECORDS

Juventus has been honoured as Serie A champions 36 times, they have been in control of Serie A for a long time now, Juventus's fanbase is the largest in Italy.

Italian Legend, Silvio Piola, has scored 274 goals in 537 Serie A match, causing him to become the all time top scorer of the Serie A.

Gianluigi Buffon (Italian) has 299 cleansheets in 658 Serie A matches, due to which he has attained the Cleansheet Leader title in the books of Serie A.

Cristiano Ronaldo (Portugese) was signed by Juventus, to fulfill their Champions League dreams, they bought him for a massive 117 million Euros from Real Madrid, making him the most expensive player ever bought into Serie A.
